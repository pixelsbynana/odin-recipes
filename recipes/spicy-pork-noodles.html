Spicy pork noodles

Description

A super spicy and satisfying noodle dish

Ingredients

  • 100g minced pork
  • 1 pack buldak ramen
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 tbsp gochujang
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp white pepper

Steps

  1. Mix minced pork with soy sauce, sugar, white pepper and gochujang
  2. Boil buldak noodles, drain and set aside
  3. Cook the pork until browned
  4. Lower the heat, pour in milk and add the Buldak seasoning packet
  5. Toss in the cooked noodles and mix well. Enjoy while hot!
