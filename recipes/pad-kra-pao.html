Pad Kra Pao

Description

A classic Thai spicy minced pork with basil leaves dish

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Light soy sauce
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Minced garlic
  • Basil leaves
  • Chillies

Steps

  1. Mince chicken breast
  2. Stir fry the minced garlic and minced chicken and add in all the sauces
  3. Serve with jasmine rice
