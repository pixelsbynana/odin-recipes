Bibimguksu

Description

A refreshing Korean spicy cold noodles, perfect for a hot summer day

Ingredients

  • Buckwheat noodles
  • Gochujang
  • Sugar
  • Minced garlic
  • Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sesame seeds
  • Boiled eggs

Steps

  1. Cook noodles and rinse in cold running water
  2. Mix with all the ingredients listed and garnish with boiled eggs
